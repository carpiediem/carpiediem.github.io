As Swimming Director at Hong Kong's Kellett School, my wife created hours of video content and dozens of instruction sets for different swimming drills. These went into great detail, to support and build consistancy among her coaching team, but it was difficult to quickly find a specific video and instructions wound up filling long documents that were difficult to read through.

I helped compile the content her team created and built a React.js app around it. This made her content more searchable and provided interactive tools for planning individual lessons from among the many drill options. To ensure that their proprietary instruction wasn't made publicaly available, access is protected with AWS Cognito user pools through a CloudFront distribution.
