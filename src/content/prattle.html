Emotics core product, Attention, used artificial intelligence to analyze webcam imagery in real-time. Separately, we used the same AI in a tool used to analyze pre-recorded videos of important speeches.

This tool supported the financial analysis of Prattle, a company specializing in examining trends in corporate and government sentiment. Our analysis could immediately flag which questions from a senate subcommittee left Jay Powell most flustered and help the finance industry predict where that would lead markets, in the future.
