Emotics core product, Attention, uses artificial intelligence to analyze webcam imagery in real-time. Separately, we use the same AI in a tool used to analyze pre-recorded videos of important speeches.

This tool supported the financial analysis of Prattle, a company specializing in examining trends in corporate and government sentiment. Our analysis immediately flagged which senator's questions left Jerome Powell most flustered and helped the finance industry predict where that would lead markets in the future.
